# AI & Marine Diesel Engines: The Future of SMART Vessels
This repository hosts the research and supplementary materials regarding the application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) for predictive maintenance in the maritime sector, with a specific focus on port tugboat operations.

## 📺 Project Overview
In this video, I provide a deep dive into my research on Prognostics and Health Management (PHM). We explore how data-driven models can outperform traditional, calendar-based maintenance by segmenting operational states to eliminate false alarms and predict mechanical failures before they occur.
